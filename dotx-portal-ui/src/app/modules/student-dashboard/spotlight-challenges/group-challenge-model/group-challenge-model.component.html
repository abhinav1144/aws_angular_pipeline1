Challenge Description: The great Turkish poet - once asked his friend ABIDIN DINO (Turkish artist and well-known painter), to draw a picture of HAPPINESS. He drew a picture of a whole family -- cramped up on a broken bed -- under a leaky roof in a shabby room -- but still with a smile on each member's face!
Goes without saying that the painting became very famous.
Yes, Happiness is not the absence of sufferings but acceptance of sufferings. See good around you even in trying situations ..stop worrying about things that are beyond your control.. stay happy...

As part of this challenge, travel through time and think of all the moments that brought you happiness. Use your creativity to share your collage of happy moments with your community.
